What is ICON Project ?

ICON Project
as its mission, “Hyperconnect the World” suggests, is a global project that seeks to create an environment in which blockchain technology is implemented in our everyday lives by connecting various blockchain networks. It is a high performance 3rd generation blockchain protocol with interoperability.
In addition to creating a platform that connects blockchain networks, ICON is simultaneously cultivating a blockchain ecosystem of its own by discovering new blockchain projects and promoting them.
ICON, beyond merely presenting blockchain projects, seeks to create brand-new services, business models, and a new paradigm by enabling those projects to interact with one another.

loopchain

loopchain, ICON’s
core blockchain engine

loopchain is a high-performance blockchain engine developed by theloop. loopchain is the private enterprise version used as the core blockchain engine of ICON, the public chain version. Thus the team of theloop and the team of ICON can be considered one and the same. It is currently leading the Korean blockchain industry by being actually utilized in various fields including financial securities, insurance, banks, universities, hospitals, and education. It is currently

Securities
  • ‘Chain ID’, the world’s first blockchain joint authentication service, has been developed on loopchain.
  • ‘Chain ID’ on smartphone is currently being operated and developed by 11 financial securities firms.
  • It will be operated with Samsung Electronics’ Samsung Pass, the default ID Management system found on every Samsung phone.
Insurance
  • In 2017, ICON and Kyobo Life launched Smart Insurance Claims service using loopchain technology.
  • This service streamlined the complicated insurance claiming process. ICON aims to expand the service beyond the current level as an automated insurance claiming service into a comprehensive insurance platform.
Bank
  • Banks on the ICON network including Woori Bank have issued prepaid electronic coins.
  • These coins allow for a variety of banking tasks including the insurance and transaction of blockchain-based digital currency and management of virtual account.
Universities
  • U-Coin is a blockchain-based virtual currency platform service that serves universities in South Korea including Sogang University and POSTECH.
  • It provides universities with convenience by offering cryptocurrency charging services that entail payments and remittances through Smart Vending Machine.
Hospitals
  • Precision Healthcare Information System (P-HIS) is the largest healthcare information consortium that ensures the integrity of leading South Korean hospitals’ data, provides a data sharing module, and develops H-sign, a blockchain-based digital signature system. ICON’s blockchain technology is being utilized in the P-HIS enterprise in order to enhance the security of medical data.
Education
  • ICON seeks to create a blockchain education ecosystem. It recently formed a strategic partnership with TRIVE, a Venture Capital firm based in Singapore, to vitalize the blockchain community education in Southeast Asia.
  • ICON is also partnering with aSSIST (Seoul School of Integrated Sciences & Technologies) Business School to develop blockchain training programs.
  • Sponsoring a monthly blockchain demo event, “Friday Night ICON – New Kids on the Blockchain,” is another avenue through which ICON engages in promoting blockchain projects.

benefit

  • Usability ICON’s loopchain technology is already being utilized in the real world, and its excellence is already being recognized.

    The technology is being implemented in various ways such as the world’s first joint authentication service, “Chain ID”, Kyobo Life’s automated insurance claiming service, and the Korea Customs Clearance Platform.

  • Scalability ICON connects not just the public blockchain, but also the private enterprise and interchain blockchain.

    Connection among blockchains, with ICON at the center, would increase the value of blockchain technology and cryptocurrency even further.

  • Reliability ICON network, which connects various communities with independent governances, is being operated on a transparent governance system.

    AI DAVinCI is going to be used for analyzing data and optimizing the incentive system. As a result, C-rep and nodes would be able to participate in the ICON community that will have become highly reliable.
